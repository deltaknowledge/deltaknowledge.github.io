Hello! I am Cheng-You Ho

I'm currently a student '25 of Chiayi Senior High School in Taiwan. My hobbies are music composition, chess and coding. I also pursue physics in my leisure time, by leading a 2024 Taiwan Young Physicists' Tournament team, doing science fairs in Physics and Astronomy, and working to be a part of the 2024 national IPhO team of Chinese Taipei.

Achievements

  • 2023 Breakthrough Junior Challenge Asia Regional Champion and Finalist.
  • 2023 Taiwan Young Physicists' Tournament Personal Award (個人獎)
  • 2023 Taiwan Regional Subject Ability Competition (學科能力競賽複賽) Second Place
